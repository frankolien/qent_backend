1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account or use our services, we may collect:

  • Identity Information: Full name, date of birth, profile photograph, and government-issued driver's license.
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Account Credentials: Email and password used to register and authenticate your account.
  • Payment Information: Bank account details, wallet balances, and transaction history processed through Paystack. We do not store your full card details on our servers.
  • Vehicle Information (Hosts): Car make, model, year, registration details, photographs, pricing, location, and availability.
  • Communications: Chat messages, voice notes, and metadata related to voice calls conducted through the Platform.
  • Verification Documents: Any additional identification documents submitted for verification.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and app version.
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, session duration, and interaction patterns.
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, access times, and crash reports.

1.3 Location Data

With your consent, we collect precise geolocation data to display nearby cars, facilitate pickup/return logistics, and provide location-based search results. You may disable location services through your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Account Management: To create, maintain, and secure your account.
  • Service Delivery: To facilitate car listings, bookings, payments, and communications between hosts and renters.
  • Payments and Payouts: To process rental payments, service fees, refunds, and host wallet payouts through Paystack.
  • Location Services: To show nearby available vehicles and support pickup/return coordination.
  • Communications: To enable in-app chat, voice notes, and voice calls between users.
  • Push Notifications: To send booking confirmations, payment updates, and reminders via Firebase Cloud Messaging.
  • Safety and Security: To detect, investigate, and prevent fraud and unauthorized access.
  • Improvements: To analyze usage patterns and improve the Platform.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3.1 With Other Users

Hosts and renters can see each other's name, profile photo, and limited contact details necessary to facilitate bookings.

3.2 With Third-Party Service Providers

  • Paystack: Payment processing and payout disbursements. See Paystack's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudinary: Secure storage of images, photos, and uploaded files. See Cloudinary's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase (Google): Push notifications and analytics. See Google's Privacy Policy.

3.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption, secure password hashing, JWT-based authentication, and access controls. Despite our efforts, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent at any time.

Contact us at support@qent.online to exercise these rights.

6. Location Data

Precise location is collected only with your explicit consent. The Platform does not collect location data while in the background unless expressly consented to. You can revoke location permissions in your device settings.

7. Communications

Chat messages are stored on our servers. Voice calls use WebRTC and are peer-to-peer — they are not recorded by Qent. Voice notes are stored via Cloudinary.

8. Payment Information

Payments are processed by Paystack (PCI DSS compliant). We do not store full payment card details. Host earnings are credited to their Qent wallet and can be withdrawn to a bank account.

9. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for individuals under 17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 17.
